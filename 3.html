Boralex Info Excellent Member Recharge Guide

Top up with Airtel/MTN

1️⃣：If you haven't registered a Boralex account yet, click the Register button on the right to sign up through my invitation link. After registering successfully, log in to your Boralex account and follow the steps to click Wallet and then Recharge to make a payment.

2️⃣: Enter the amount you want to recharge and click "Confirm" at the bottom.

3️⃣: Choose "MTN" or "Airtel".

——————————————————

4️⃣: Whether you're using MTN or Airtel, once you enter the transaction page, click the copy button to copy the number of the staff from Boralex's finance department.

5️⃣: After copying the number, go back to the phone dialing page, enter *115#and click the dial button. Then follow the steps to transfer funds to the account of Boralex's finance department.

——————————————————

6️⃣: Whether you use MTN or Airtel for payment, after you complete the payment, you'll receive a "transaction SMS"from MTN or Airtel. Copy the transaction ID in the text message, then go back to Boralex's transaction page, Enter the transaction ID in the input box, and then click "Submit or CONFIRM" to complete the payment.

After your payment is successful

After the payment is successful, please click the [Contact] button on the right side of the page to reach out to me. I will patiently guide and assist you, and add you to our Boralex WhatsApp work group.
