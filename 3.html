Top up with Airtel/MTN

1️⃣: Click [Log in to the Boralex account] to log in to your Boralex account. On the "Wallet" page, click "Recharge".

2️⃣: Enter the amount you want to recharge and click "Confirm" at the bottom.

3️⃣: Confirm the number you use for the transaction, then click "confirm".

4️⃣: After clicking "confirm", you'll enter Boralex's transaction page. Then copy the account number of the staff in Boralex's finance department. After copying the number, go back to the phone dialing page, enter *115#and click the dial button. Then follow the steps to transfer funds to the account of Boralex's finance department.

5️⃣: Whether you use MTN or Airtel for payment, after you complete the payment, you'll receive a "transaction SMS"from MTN or Airtel. Copy the entire content of the transaction SMS, then go back to Boralex's transaction page, click "Refresh", paste the transaction SMS here, and then click "Submit" to complete the payment.

After your payment is successful

After your payment is successful, please click the customer service button or contact the friend who invited you to join Boralex. By clicking the customer service button, you can get in touch with the Boralex manager. She will teach you how to rent a machine, how to claim your daily earnings, how to withdraw your income, and will guide you to earn more in the future.
