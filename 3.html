Top up with Airtel/MTN

1️⃣: Click https://boralex-zm.com to log in to your Boralex account. On the "Wallet" page, click "Recharge".

2️⃣: Enter the amount you want to recharge and click "Confirm" at the bottom.

3️⃣: Select your payment method (MTN or Airtel) and then click "Select".

4️⃣: When you enter the payment page, you will see the MTN or Airtel number of the "Boralex Finance Department Staff". Copy this number, then go back to your phone's dial - up page and dial "*115#". Follow the prompts to complete the payment.

5️⃣: After the payment is completed, you will receive a transaction message from "MTN or Airtel". The transaction message contains a transaction ID. Fill in the transaction ID on the Boralex payment page and click "Submit" to finish the process.

After your payment is successful

After your payment is successful, please click the customer service button or contact the friend who invited you to join Boralex. By clicking the customer service button, you can get in touch with the Boralex manager. She will teach you how to rent a machine, how to claim your daily earnings, how to withdraw your income, and will guide you to earn more in the future.
